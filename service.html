OUR SERVICE

With a large media, and some media customs uncommon to multinational clients, the Matterhorn team has worked tirelessly to develop strong relationships with local and locally based international press in Vietnam.

Depending on what you count, Vietnam's media scape includes up to 1200 print publications, a fast growing TV scape as locally based cable television gains traction, and an exploding online environment that now sees around 26 million of the nation's population using the internet regularly.

It takes a trusted partner to walk a client through the shifting sands of Vietnam's media, and Matterhorn – named for one of Europe's largest mountains – is just the sherpa you need..
